KAILA HUNLEY

KAILA HUNLEY IS A SENIOR FROM PHOENIX, ARIZONA GRADUATING MAGNA CUM LAUDE WITH A BA IN MULTIMEDIA CONCENTRATING IN GRAPHIC DESIGN. KAILA IS INSPIRED BY NATURE, MOVEMENT, AND HER FRIENDS AND FAMILY. SHE IS ALSO THE CAPTAIN OF THE CLU DANCE TEAM AND GAINS INSPIRATION FROM THE CONNECTIONS SHE MAKES THROUGH LEADING THE DANCE TEAM AS WELL AS CREATING AND CHOREOGRAPHING FOR THEM. HER FAVORITE MEDIUMS TO WORK WITH ARE ILLUSTRATOR, INDESIGN, AND PROCREATE. AFTER GRADUATION, KAILA PLANS TO RETURN TO ARIZONA AND ACCEPT AN ASSISTANT OR HEAD DANCE COACH POSITION AT A LOCAL HIGH SCHOOL, TEACH AT HER CHILDHOOD DANCE STUDIO, AND EVENTUALLY PURSUE A MASTERS DEGREE IN INTERIOR DESIGN.
