KAITLYN PARR

KAITLYN PARR IS FROM LAKEWOOD, WA, AND MAJORS IN MULTIMEDIA WITH AN EMPHASIS IN BOTH GRAPHIC DESIGN AND ANIMATION. SHE IS INSPIRED BY ANIMATED FILMS, POP CULTURE, AND HER EXPERIENCES FROM GROWING UP IN THE PACIFIC NORTHWEST. SHE ENJOYS DESIGNING FOR BOTH PRINT AND DIGITAL MEDIA WITH ADOBE ILLUSTRATOR, INDESIGN, AND PROCREATE. OUTSIDE OF UNIVERSITY, KAITLYN LOVES READING, PRACTICING YOGA, AND SPENDING TIME WITH FRIENDS.
