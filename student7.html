ALEXANDER CHIRIKIAN

ALEX, FROM ARCADIA, CALIFORNIA, IS MAJORING IN MULTIMEDIA WITH AN EMPHASIS IN MOTION GRAPHICS, VISUAL EFFECTS, AND ANIMATION. ANIMATED FILMS AND TV SHOWS INSPIRE HIM AS HE IS FASCINATED BY THE ANIMATION QUALITY AS WELL AS THE STORYTELLING. HIS FAVORITE TYPE OF MEDIA IS ANIMATED TV SHOWS, SUCH AS INVINCIBLE, STAR WARS: THE CLONE WARS, AND BATMAN THE ANIMATED SERIES. HIS GOAL IS TO ANIMATE SIMILAR CONTENT IN HIS FUTURE CAREER.
